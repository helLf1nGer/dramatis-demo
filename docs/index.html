How the studio made it

Not a TTS call — a Director running a session

Everything below is what the AI Director actually did to produce the Marley piece above: 166 reasoning steps over typed tools, specialist sub-agents, and an observe-then-repair loop. No human edits.

director · analyze_text

Read the manuscript, found its shape

Broke the 301-word passage into four dramatic waves — the knocker becoming a face, the ascent through empty rooms, the bells & the rising chain, and the confrontation — across 20 lines and 3 speaking parts.

audition_voice → evaluate_audition → cast_voice

Auditioned 9 voices, cast a 3-register ensemble

Sampled candidate voices for each role, had every take evaluated for character fit and ensemble contrast, then cast for a deliberate clash of registers — high, mid, deep. The verdicts:

Scrooge → Orus“brilliantly high, thin and dry”
Marley → Algenib“weary, sepulchral… tolling-bell weight”
Narrator → Charon“warm fireside Dickensian storyteller”
set_audio_treatment → synthesize_batch

Directed the performance

Gave each character a tailored treatment — Marley a genuinely supernatural reverb + pitch-drop, the narrator a warm polish — then performed all 19 line-level stems.

dispatch_worker ×3 · in parallel

Handed off to a review crew — all at once

Dispatched three specialists concurrently, each its own sub-agent with its own tools:

QA engineerTranscribes every line back, measures pacing + dialogue masking.
Creative dramaturg“Scrooge's arc perfectly realized… the register clash superb.”
Sound designerBuilds the four-scene cinematic soundscape.
observe → repair → re-verify

Caught its own mistakes and fixed them

QA transcribed the rendered audio back, found genuine flubs and a narrator take that drifted sharp, re-took just those lines, and re-checked — distinguishing real defects from false alarms by ear:

transcript86.5%→99.7% voice consistency0/2→2/3 dialogue masking−4.4 dB→clear
design_soundscape → mix_chapter → export

Sound-designed and mastered

Placed 16 cues across the four waves — bitter wind & a distant bell → the door-slam echoing through an empty house → one bell swelling into every bell, dragging chains, the cellar-door boom → spectral resonance and a sepulchral cello. Mixed 42 layers at a radio-drama profile; two independent mix reviews returned “release-ready.”
